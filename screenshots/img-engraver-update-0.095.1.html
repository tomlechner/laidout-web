Engraver Tool, part 2
A demonstration of several more of the new engraver tool features. This one includes new direction options for the engraver tool, which allows a access to linear, circular, radial and now spiral lines without having to know keyboard shortcuts. Also a brief description of the new ability to share settings between different line groups and engraver objects
